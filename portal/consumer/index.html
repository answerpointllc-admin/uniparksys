Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a reservation?

Go to My Reservations → click Cancel. Free cancellation 2+ hours before start. 50% refund within 2 hours. No refund after start time.

What happens if I overstay?

15-minute grace period, then overstay fees at 2× the standard rate per 15-minute increment. Push notification sent at expiry.

Can I book an EV spot with a non-EV vehicle?

No. EV charging spots require an EV-registered vehicle on your account. Add your EV in My Vehicles with the "EV Registered" designation.

How far in advance can I book?

Up to 30 days in advance. Maximum single reservation duration is 72 hours. 15-minute buffer enforced between consecutive bookings.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All payments processed via Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1). We never store full card numbers. Digital wallet support for Apple Pay and Google Pay.
