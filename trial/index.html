Trial FAQ

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to the Consumer Portal and Client Portal with all features unlocked, including dynamic pricing, IoT sensor views, EV management, and reporting. Your environment is pre-loaded with 3 demo facilities and realistic booking data.

Do I need a credit card?

No. The trial is completely free with no credit card required. There is no auto-billing at the end of the trial period.

Can I extend my trial?

Yes. You can request a one-time 7-day extension from your trial dashboard, giving you a total of 22 days to evaluate UniPark.

What happens when the trial ends?

Your trial environment is archived for 30 days. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time to retain your configuration. Demo data is not migrated — you start fresh with your real facilities.

What's the difference between the tiers?

Lite ($299/mo) covers basic parking operations for up to 3 facilities. Regular ($799/mo) adds dynamic pricing, IoT sensors, EV management, and the Local Admin portal for up to 15 facilities. Enterprise is fully customizable with unlimited scale, Global Admin, API access, and white-label branding.

Is printing available during the trial?

No. Printing (receipts, reports, invoices) is disabled during the trial period. Full printing capabilities are enabled on all paid plans.

Can I bring my own data?

During the trial, you work with pre-loaded demo data. On a paid plan, our onboarding team helps you import your real facilities, pricing rules, and historical data.
