How It Works

From parked to found in four simple steps

1

Park & Open

Park your car and open the UniPark app. Enter your reservation key or scan the QR code.

2

Connect

The app connects to the parking facility's sensor network and locates your exact spot.

3

Navigate

Follow the compass pointer. Christopher guides you with voice directions and distance updates.

4

Arrive

The app confirms when you've reached your vehicle. Trip summary with distance and time.
